Vignan University

Established in 2009, Vignan's Foundation for Science, Technology, and Research (VFSTR) University, (Vignan University) is a Deemed to be University. It is approved by the University Grants Commission (UGC), and has been accredited with grade A+ by National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC).

Courses offered include Chemical Engineering, Food Technology, agricultural engineering, automobile engineering, bioinformatics, biomedical engineering, biotechnology, chemical engineering, civil engineering, COMPUTER SCIENCE AND ENGINEERING , electrical & electronics engineering, electronics & communications engineering, information technology, mechanical engineering, petroleum engineering and textile technology.
